What is Esprit Business Forum?










The Esprit Business Forum, hosted by Esprit University in Tunisia, is an event focused on bringing together students, academics, professionals, and industry leaders to discuss and explore various aspects of business, entrepreneurship, and innovation. These forums typically feature a range of activities such as keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, networking sessions, and possibly even exhibitions or competitions. The aim is to provide participants with opportunities to learn from experienced professionals, gain insights into the latest trends and developments in the business world, and connect with potential mentors, collaborators, or employers. The Esprit Business Forum serves as a platform for exchanging ideas, sharing experiences, and fostering collaboration among individuals interested in business and entrepreneurship. It can be a valuable opportunity for students to expand their knowledge, network with industry professionals, and explore potential career paths or entrepreneurial ventures.
