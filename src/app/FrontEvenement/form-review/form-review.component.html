What is Esprit business Forum?

Esprit Business forum is a twice a year event organized by the university to bring together business professionals, entrepreneurs, academics, and students to discuss various aspects of business, entrepreneurship, and technology. The forum include panel discussions, keynote speeches, workshops, and networking sessions aimed at fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing within the business community. For precise information about the Esprit Business Forum, it would be best to consult event organizers.
